Veronica

Veronica is an autonomous robot that was designed and built by myself and 3 others during the summer of 2017. She competed in the ENPH 259 "Mission Impossi-bots" Competition and placed in the top half of the class. I was primarily responsible for Computer Aided Design on parts and assemblies and software development. Read more about the competition details, Veronica, and my contributions to the project at channel4robotteam.github.io.
